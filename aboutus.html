Accreditation

The Directorate of Industrial Training (DIT) is a Ugandan quality assurance directorate offering nationally, regionally and internationally recognized quality assurance services for Business, Technical, Vocational Education and Training. The certificates offered by GBL through DIT are recognized and meet the standards of education and training nationally, regionally and internationally.

The certificates obtained by students at GBL Upskilling and Mentoring Centre enable them to get jobs from many parts of the world due to the high standards they posses.

All students who complete their courses and pass the final examination set by DIT are awarded a certificate to show that they have mastered the skill that they were learning.
